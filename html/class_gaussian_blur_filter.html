Construct an gaussian blur kernel with radius size, strength sd.

An identity kernel with a dimension ( size * 2 - 1) * ( size * 2 - 1) is constructed. The weight of each entry in the kernel is generated by the gaussian distribution, with center as the mean, and standard deviation sd.

Parameters
sizeSize/radius of the kernel.
sdStrength/standard deviation of the distribution.
